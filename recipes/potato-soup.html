Potato Soup

Description

Nothing settles the belly after a long day like potato soup. Thick, creamy, and hearty, this soup is sure to please.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 1 small carrot, diced
  • 1 tbsp herbs de provence
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 6 cloves garlic, sliced
  • 4 cups vegetable stock
  • 7oz smoked cheddar cheese or normal cheddar + ½ tsp smoked paprika
  • ½ cup sour cream
