Grilled Cheese

Description

Who doesn't love a grilled cheese? Cripsy, crunchy, and just the right amount of ooey-gooiness make this sandwich a staple of childhood. This twist on a classic uses gochujang to add some sweet spiciness.

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons gochujang, or more to taste
  • 4 slices white bread
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 4 slices cheese

Steps

  1. Spread the gochujang on one side each of 2 slices of bread.
  2. Add 2 tablespoons butter to a large nonstick pan over medium-high heat. Swirl the melting butter around the pan to coat.
  3. Add the 2 pieces of prepared bread to the pan, gochujang-side up. Move them around to absorb the melted butter.
  4. Layer 2 pieces of cheese on each slice and top each with the final 2 pieces of bread.
  5. Cook on one side until a peek at the underside reveals a golden brown crust.
  6. Flip carefully, adding the remaining 2 tablespoons butter to the pan and tucking it underneath the sandwiches.
  7. Cook until the other side is golden.
  8. Cut in half and serve.
